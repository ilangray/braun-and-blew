In 2005, Hurricane Katrina (below) devastated the city of New Orleans. Over eighteen hundred people died and more than one hundred billion dollars worth of damage was inflicted on the city due to failing infrastructure and the raw power of the oceans.

This visualization will teach you about the lifecycle of hurricanes and the idiosyncratic features of the natural world that cause hurricanes live the way they do. You will also be able to filter and explores past hurricanes in order to discover your own trends.

This is how hurricanes are born. Look, they start down by Africa. We put a circle around them. Yay.
